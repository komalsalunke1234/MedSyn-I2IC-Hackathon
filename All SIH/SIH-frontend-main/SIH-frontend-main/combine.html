Health Services and Treatment Options

  • Maternity Care +

    - Regular prenatal checkups are vital for monitoring baby's growth and mother's health.
    - Maintain a balanced diet, stay hydrated, and get moderate exercise.
    - Avoid stress, smoking, and alcohol for a healthy pregnancy.

  • Newborn Care +

    - Ensure skin-to-skin contact and breastfeeding within the first hour after birth.
    - Keep the baby warm, clean, and dry.
    - Monitor baby's feeding and diaper output to track health.

  • Mother's Health Postpartum +

    - Take proper rest and follow a nutritious diet to aid recovery.
    - Monitor for postpartum depression or unusual symptoms.
    - Attend follow-up appointments and consider pelvic floor exercises.

  • Infant Vaccination +

    - Follow the national immunization schedule strictly.
    - Timely vaccinations protect from serious diseases.
    - Keep a vaccination record card updated.

  • Breastfeeding Support +

    - Exclusive breastfeeding is recommended for the first 6 months.
    - Ensures baby gets essential nutrients and antibodies.
    - Seek lactation consultant help for feeding challenges.

  • Mother & Baby Nutrition +

    - Mothers need iron, calcium, and protein-rich diets during and after pregnancy.
    - Babies should get breast milk or formula; avoid solids before 6 months.
    - Monitor for allergies when introducing new foods.

  • Safety & Hygiene +

    - Always wash hands before handling the baby.
    - Ensure the sleeping area is clean, safe, and clutter-free.
    - Use mild, baby-safe hygiene products.
